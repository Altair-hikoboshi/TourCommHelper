Common Tourist Expressions

  • How much is this? - Berapa harganya ini?
  • Where is the nearest restroom? - Di mana kamar mandi terdekat?
  • Can you tell me about this place? - Bisakah Anda memberi tahu saya tentang tempat ini?
  • How do I get to the museum? - Bagaimana cara saya ke museum?
  • I need help. - Saya butuh bantuan.
